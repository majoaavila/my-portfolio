Her Code

Graphic design & Marketing

Her Code is a community of women aimed at facilitating knowledge and experience exchange among female students.
The community's focus lies in cultivating an inclusive environment that nurtures professional growth, confidence, and leadership.
Efforts are directed towards overcoming barriers, driving diversity and representation, and championing industry excellence.
This, in turn, encourages active and sustainable participation of women in the technology sector.

Overall, the creation of Her Code's branding identity was a process of meticulous design thinking, strategic alignment with our values, and an aspiration to visually communicate our dedication to empowering women in technology.
